📊 Key Insights

Standard service level generates the highest total revenue

Certain carriers handle the majority of shipments

Long-distance shipments tend to have higher costs

Delivery delays vary across carriers and service levels

Some routes are significantly more expensive than others

💡 Business Recommendations

Focus on high-revenue service levels to maximize profit

Optimize high-cost routes and logistics operations

Improve carrier performance for better delivery efficiency

Promote premium delivery services for higher revenue
